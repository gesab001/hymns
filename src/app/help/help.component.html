HOW TO USE IN DESKTOP

CLICK on the lyrics to change to the next verse or SWIPE LEFT

SWIPE RIGHT to go back to previous verse

HOW TO USE IN TOUCH SCREEN DEVICES

TAP or SWIPE LEFT to change to the next verse

SWIPE RIGHT to go back to previous verse

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS

RIGHT ARROW or UP ARROW = next verse

LEFT ARROW or DOWN ARROW = previous verse

hymn number + ENTER = jump to a hymn
